Vrygrond Interviews

Published by ben on August 30, 2018

Spread the love

I got the opportunity to go into Vrygrond and interview some people at Where Rainbows Meet. This was both an insightful and heartbreaking experience. I got one step closer to actually understanding what others have gone through.

Below is an interview I held with Roger Daims, the senior cook at Where Rainbows Meet. We discussed the role of Where Rainbows Meet and some key factors which contribute to the poverty in Vrygrond.

 

This interview I held with Veronica, one of the women from the sewing department at Rainbows. We talked about her personal life and some of the issues in Vrygrond.

Here, I interviewed Shahram Jacobs, another women from Rainbow’s sewing department. She brought some valuable insight into the issues in Vrygrond and what is most needed as help.
