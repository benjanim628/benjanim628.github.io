About

Spread the love

Here at Nurturing Hope we try and make a difference to the lives of less fortunate individuals. To achieve this goal it takes a lot of work and the monitory donations of you, our loved supporters.

Our most urgent project at the moment is the support of the young children enrolled in the Early Child Development program at Where Rainbows Meet. These children are from families where unemployment takes a big toll, leaving them without much support. This program allows children to grow in a nourishing and enriching environment instead of being left at home possibly without much food and care.
