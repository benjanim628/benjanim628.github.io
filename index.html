About Author

I am Benjamin Janowski a grade 12 student at Constantia Waldorf School.  

At Waldorf the curriculum is unique in the way that every student is given the chance to go on a journey in the 12’th grade. This allows the student to learn new things and challenge themselves in new ways to allow for a broader learning experience.  

I have chosen to devote my project to learning about, and helping local communities, particularly those in underprivileged circumstances. I got to know a Non-Profit organization called Where Rainbows Meet where the most phenomenal things happen. Every week day more than 350 people are given nutritious meals while over 60 children from ages 3-8 are given a roof over their heads. These future leaders are given a safe space to play and a teacher to care for them, being separated from daily harsh conditions.   Not all children are able to afford this daily care but this is where the rest of us come into play.

We, the healthy leaders of today can make a difference in our community. Each step towards a better world counts, we will leave our marks behind today.
